Pizza



Description

Pizza is a traditional italian dish loved by all manner of people and New Yorkers.

It is a very tasty dish, just try not to eat to much of it, it's a very calory dense meal!.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Let it rise
  3. Knead the dough into a pizza shape
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza evenly
  5. Put cheese on top
  6. Bake in the oven
