Lasagna



Description

Lasagna is a traditional italian dish loved by all manner of people and cartoon animals alike.

It is a very tasty dish, just try not to eat to much of it, it's a very calory dense meal!.


Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Build the lasagna
  4. Put cheese on top
  5. Bake in the oven
